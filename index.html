Common questions about our phone repair services.

Most common repairs like screen or battery replacements take about 30-60 minutes. More complex repairs may take longer, but we'll always give you an estimated time when you bring in your device. Many repairs can be completed while you wait!
Yes, we offer warranties on all our repairs and parts. Screen replacements come with a 30-day warranty, batteries have a 6-month warranty, and other repairs have a 3-month warranty. We stand behind our work and will fix any issues that arise during the warranty period.
We use high-quality OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) parts or premium aftermarket parts that meet or exceed original specifications. We'll always inform you about the parts we're using and their warranty coverage before starting any repair.
If we determine that your phone cannot be repaired, we'll explain why and provide you with options. We can help you recover your data and offer trade-in options for a new device. We also sell quality refurbished phones at competitive prices.
